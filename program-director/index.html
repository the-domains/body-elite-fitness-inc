Frank DeJesus holds body building titles which include Mr. New Jersey, Mr. East Coast and Mr. Western USA. He is certified with the National Academy of Sports Medicine and the International Sports Sciences Association. He is also a member of the American College of Sports Medicine and a professional member of the National Strength and Conditioning Association, as well as certified with the American Heart Association and Red Cross for CPR and AED programs. Mr. DeJesus has been a martial arts expert for over 20 years. He is a certified second degree black belt with World Oyama Karate and is a first degree black belt in Hapkido and trained in Muay Thai Boxing.
